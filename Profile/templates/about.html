Presenting the Dominator 77 Cricket Team



About Us

Dominator 77 is a cricket team from the D-77 batch in the Computer Science and Engineering department at Dhaka International University. Founded in 2024, we have quickly become well-known in our batch. Our team is made up of skilled and passionate players who strive for excellence and good sportsmanship, embodying the strong unity and spirit of our D-77 batch. Together, we aim to bring pride and success to our team and our batch.

Our Mission

Our mission is to dominate the cricket field with exceptional performance, fostering a culture of teamwork, integrity, and continuous improvement. We aim to inspire and engage our community through our dedication to the sport and our pursuit of excellence.
